MyFitnessPal Redesign

I went through an end-to-end process of redesigning the popular calorie tracking app, MyFitnessPal. I conducted user research, wireframed and prototyped a new solution to the problems I found with the current iOS application.

Designlab - UX Academy

Designlab, a company offering mentored online design courses, released a new product offering called UX Academy. With this, they introduced two new levels to the course architecture. I was responsible for handling the user experience related to adding these new levels into the existing course structure and layout.

Minted - Storefront Tool

Designers on Minted have a storefront where all their products are visible. I designed and implemented a new tool that would allow them to customize the first three items appearing in their store.

Google Maps - Feature Concept
